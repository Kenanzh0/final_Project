SYMPTOMS

1. Fever: Elevated body temperature.

2. Dry cough: Coughing without mucus.

3. Fatigue: Feeling tired and exhausted.

4. Shortness of breath: Difficulty breathing.

5. Loss of smell or taste: Loss or alteration of the sense of smell or taste.

6. Muscle pain: Pain in muscles and joints.

7. Sore throat: Feeling of pain or congestion in the throat.

8. Headache: Experiencing headaches.

9. Chills: Feeling cold or experiencing shivers.

10. Nausea or vomiting: Feeling nauseous or the urge to vomit.

11. Diarrhea: Having watery or loose stools.

PREVENTION METHODS

1. Vaccination: Get the recommended vaccines as advised by health authorities.

2. Wearing masks: Especially in indoor and crowded places.

3. Hand washing: Regularly and thoroughly with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.

4. Using sanitizers: That contain at least 60% alcohol when soap and water are not available.

5. Social distancing: Maintaining a safe distance of at least one meter (3 feet) from others.

6. Avoiding crowds: And large gatherings and celebrations.

7. Ventilating spaces: Ensuring that indoor areas are well-ventilated.

8. Covering mouth and nose: When coughing or sneezing with a tissue or a bent elbow.

9. Avoid touching face: Especially eyes, nose, and mouth with unwashed hands.

10. Staying home: When feeling any flu-like symptoms or when confirmed to be infected.
